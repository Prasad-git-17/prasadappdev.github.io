Privacy Policy

Invoice Maker – PDF Billing App

Effective Date: March 29, 2026

Introduction

By using the app, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

Invoice Maker – PDF Billing App ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We do not directly collect personal or non-personal information from users. However, certain third-party services used in the app (such as Google Play Billing and RevenueCat) may process limited data required to provide their functionality.

Purchase information may be used to restore purchases across devices or app reinstalls.

  • No login or signup required
  • No user accounts
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising identifiers

All invoice data, including text, numbers, and signatures, is stored locally on your device using an SQLite database.

In-App Purchases

The app offers optional in-app purchases for premium features. These purchases are processed securely through third-party services.

  • Payments are handled by Google Play Billing
  • We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions and purchases
  • We do not collect or store your payment information
  • Google may collect information such as purchase history, device identifiers, and account details to process transactions
  • RevenueCat may process anonymous purchase data and app usage related to subscriptions

All payment-related data is handled according to Google’s and RevenueCat’s privacy policies.

All purchases are final and managed by Google Play. Refunds are subject to Google Play's refund policies.

Local Data Storage

  • All invoice data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not have access to your data
  • You have full control to create, edit, export, or delete your invoices
  • Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data

Optional Google Drive Backup

The app will request access to your Google Drive account only when you choose to create a backup. No data is accessed or uploaded without your explicit action. The app provides an optional feature that allows users to manually back up their invoice data to their personal Google Drive account.

  • This feature is optional and initiated by the user
  • Backup data is stored in the Google Drive appDataFolder, which is private to the app and the user’s account
  • The developer of this app cannot access or view this data
  • Data stored in Google Drive is subject to Google’s privacy policies

Signature Data

Any signature added by the user is stored locally on the device or within the user's backup file and is never shared with the developer.

Third-Party Services

The app may interact with third-party services in the following cases:

  • Google Drive (for optional backup)
  • Google Play Billing (for in-app purchases)
  • RevenueCat (for managing subscriptions and purchases)

For more details:

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

RevenueCat Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party services to understand how your data is handled.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect personal data from children. However, third-party services such as Google Play Billing and RevenueCat may process limited data required for purchases. We recommend parental supervision when purchases are enabled.

Data Security

We do not operate any backend servers to store user data.

All invoice data is stored locally on the device or within the user's personal Google Drive account. We do not transmit or store your data on our own servers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app or on the Play Store listing.
