Privacy Policy

Invoice Maker – PDF Billing App

Effective Date: March 9, 2026

Introduction

Invoice Maker App ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal or non-personal information.

  • No login or signup required
  • No user accounts
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising identifiers

All invoice data, including text, numbers, and signatures, is stored locally on your device using an SQLite database.

Local Data Storage

  • All invoice data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not have access to your data
  • You have full control to create, edit, export, or delete your invoices
  • Uninstalling the app will remove all locally stored data

Optional Google Drive Backup

The app provides an optional feature that allows users to manually back up their invoice data to their personal Google Drive account.

  • This feature is optional and initiated by the user
  • Backup data is stored in the Google Drive appDataFolder, which is private to the app and the user’s account
  • The developer of this app cannot access or view this data
  • Data stored in Google Drive is subject to Google’s privacy policies

Signature Data

Any signature added by the user is stored locally on the device or within the user's backup file and is never shared with the developer.

Third-Party Services

If the user chooses to use the backup feature, the app will interact with Google Drive services solely for storing the user's backup file. This app does not use services for analytics, advertising, or tracking.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any personal data from children. Since the app does not collect user data, there is no risk of data misuse.

Data Security

All invoice data is stored locally on the device or within the user's personal Google Drive account. We do not transmit or store your data on our own servers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app or on the Play Store listing.
